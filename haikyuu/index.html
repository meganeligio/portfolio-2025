HAIKYUU!!

  • UX/UI
  • Social Network

Overview

HAIKYUU!! was a social networking mobile app that I created for the series because I thought it would be fun to dedicate a project to my favorite anime and manga. The focal point of the concept was to provide a main outlet for Haikyu!! enthusiasts to express themselves and to bring the community together.

splash screen

Problem

My common issue when it came to fandom communities was content being scattered across various sites with some being more populated than others. With Haikyu!! being my favorite series, I wanted to tackle this problem us fellow fans to gather in one huge space where we could freely share our love and enjoyment for the anime and manga.

Solution

I designed a mobile app where I implemented engaging key features that would provide users an entertaining experience. They could hold discussions, post fan‐arts, download custom app icons and wallpapers, etc. It encouraged fans to gravitate towards the app more if they strictly wanted Haikyu!! content. I used visuals official chibi illustrations of some fan‐favorite characters.
