Student Project

ANI BLITZ!

  • UX/UI
  • Puzzle Game

Overview

ANI BLITZ! was a concept game that I thought of after seeing a friend play a puzzle game. It inspired me to use this idea for a project because I wanted to challenge myself with creating my own game. I revolved it around anime since I loved watching it and I was most familiar with the community. The project's focal point was to develop a game for anime fans—such as myself—to enjoy playing.

splash screen

Problem

There were many games dedicated to a specific anime, but rarely were there ones that had fun crossovers between characters from different anime series'. I wanted to take matters into my own hands and address this problem in a way that was appeasing to the fan community.

Solution

I developed a puzzle game where users could play with a variety of fan‐favorite characters from multiple anime series'. I incorporated key features such as a leaderboard, collection tab, and background music featuring songs that were favored or popular within the anime community. The gameplay was as simple as connecting three or more characters and using special abilities. I wanted the players to have a cozy yet engaging experience.
